Personal Bio

I studied at the best school in our city – Gymnasium No. 1 (Partner School of the Russian Academy of Sciences). In addition to it, from 2022 to 2024, in order to obtain the Canadian Ontario Secondary School Diploma (OSSD), I have been studying in a joint online academic credit program between Red Leaf Group and Ontario e-School called “Edualia” in the Engineering and Architecture Stream program.
During the education process, I gained useful experience in teamwork while creating the project “Unmanned aerial vehicle for observing animals in the wild.” At that time, I was responsible for creating project drawings and mastered computer drawing skills. I also successfully completed online courses in the field of Frontend developer and I have gained my skills in designing websites. In addition, in order to successfully pass exams at the Gymnasium and receive a gold medal “For exceptional academic achievements,” I took paid online courses in computer science.
Currently studying at Humber Polytechnic on Computer Programming and Analysis program. I hope that by studying here I will be able to gain advanced academic knowledge, as well as develop innovative thinking, and gain practical skills in applying my knowledge.

Due to good education, I have quite a lot of skills in different areas. I am sure that I am able to apply these skills well due to my determination and some other personal qualities, such as patience and responsibility, for example.
